My KNES381 Journey

This website serves as a demonstration of what I've learned in the class, KNES381! Join me as I showcase various documents and works that encapsulate this journey.

Excel Spreadsheet

Below are two separate Excel sheets. The first is the Excel sheet that I made in class for the Excel project. The second sheet depicts my personal blood pressure data, which I collected over 5 days using different devices, to see if they had different results.

My (totally real) Hiking Trip

In this project, I replaced my landscape with a new one, excised a person that was in that landscape, and inserted myself into the picture! I also changed the color levels of each photo to blend together.
